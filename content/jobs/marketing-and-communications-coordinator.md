# Marketing & Communications Coordinator

## About the Role

NexusPoint Systems is seeking a talented Marketing & Communications Coordinator to join our growing team and help position us as Western Australia's premier security-first managed service provider. Reporting directly to Managing Director Alex Nguyen, this part-time role (24 hours per week) is perfect for a marketing professional looking to make a significant impact in a dynamic, tech-focused environment.

As we expand our cybersecurity capabilities and pursue larger enterprise contracts, we need someone who can translate our technical expertise into compelling content that resonates with mid-market business leaders. You'll be the voice behind our digital presence, working closely with our Cybersecurity Lead Marcus Webb and Head of Service Delivery Priya Sharma to showcase our security-first approach.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Content Creation**: Develop case studies, whitepapers, and blog posts that demonstrate our cybersecurity expertise and successful client outcomes
- **Social Media Management**: Manage our LinkedIn presence with regular posts highlighting industry insights, team achievements, and thought leadership
- **Email Marketing**: Create and distribute monthly newsletters to our client base and prospect database
- **Website Content**: Collaborate with our team to keep website content fresh, accurate, and aligned with our security-first positioning
- **Marketing Collateral**: Design and produce sales materials, service sheets, and presentation templates for our business development efforts
- **Event Support**: Coordinate our participation in local business events and industry conferences
- **Brand Management**: Ensure consistent messaging across all channels that positions NexusPoint as the trusted cybersecurity partner for WA businesses

## What You'll Bring

- **Experience**: 2-4 years in marketing communications, preferably in B2B tech or professional services
- **Content Skills**: Strong writing abilities with experience creating technical content for business audiences
- **Digital Marketing**: Proficiency with LinkedIn, email marketing platforms, and content management systems
- **Design Capabilities**: Basic graphic design skills using tools like Canva or Adobe Creative Suite
- **Industry Knowledge**: Understanding of cybersecurity concepts and managed IT services (or willingness to learn quickly)
- **Communication**: Excellent written and verbal communication skills with attention to detail
- **Initiative**: Self-starter who can work independently while collaborating effectively with technical teams
- **Local Knowledge**: Familiarity with the WA business landscape is highly valued

## What We Offer

- **Competitive Salary**: $25-30 per hour based on experience
- **Flexible Schedule**: 24 hours per week with flexible timing to suit your lifestyle
- **Growth Opportunity**: Join us during an exciting expansion phase with potential for increased hours and responsibilities
- **Learning Environment**: Work directly with cybersecurity experts and gain valuable industry knowledge
- **Modern Workspace**: Our Subiaco office features standing desks, quality coffee, and a collaborative atmosphere
- **Professional Development**: Budget for courses, certifications, and conference attendance
- **Team Culture**: Join a close-knit team of 35 professionals who value work-life balance and continuous improvement

## How to Apply

Ready to help us tell the NexusPoint story? Send your resume and a brief cover letter explaining why you're excited about this role to [careers@nexuspointsystems.com.au](mailto:careers@nexuspointsystems.com.au). Please include "Marketing Coordinator Application" in the subject line.

We'd love to see examples of your work, so feel free to include links to content you've created, social media accounts you've managed, or marketing campaigns you've been involved with.

Applications close Friday, 15th December 2024. We're looking to fill this position in early January, with an immediate start available for the right candidate.

*NexusPoint Systems is an equal opportunity employer committed to creating an inclusive workplace for all team members.*
